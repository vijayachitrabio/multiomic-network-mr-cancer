Multi-omic triangulation of circulating proteins identifies novel breast cancer causal candidates.

Modhukur V et al. 2026

View on GitHub

Key findings
  • 701 circulating proteins screened; cis-pQTL instruments from FinnGen Olink (N = 619).
  • 17 protein–cancer associations survived BH-FDR correction.
  • 5 Tier 1 (MR + dual coloc): EFNA1, TNFRSF6B, ATRAID, FGF5, ABO.
  • 3 Tier 2a (MR + ABF-only coloc): IL34, SWAP70, UMOD.
  • Robust replication across ARIC, OpenGWAS, and independent clinical datasets.
